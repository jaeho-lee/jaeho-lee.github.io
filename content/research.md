## research

for an extended overview, see the [group webpage](https://effl.postech.ac.kr).

***

My long-term goal is to make AI more ***responsible*** --- accessible, sustainable, and righteous.

To this end, I currently focus on ***AI efficiency***.

In particular, I aim to understand the fundamental trade-offs among three key factors:
- predictive performance
- inference cost
- training cost

I believe that such an understanding can be gained by studying the ***algorithmic biases*** inherent in machine learning algorithms.
